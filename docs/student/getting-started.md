## Your Dashboard

After signing in, you land on your personal **Dashboard** (`/dashboard`). It is designed to give you a quick overview of what is happening in the community:

- **Teachers that might suit you** — a scrollable carousel of teachers you are connected with. Click "Show all teachers" to browse the full member directory filtered by the Teacher role.
- **Courses you might enjoy** — a carousel of recently published courses from the Academy. Click "Show all courses" to view the full catalogue.
- **Upcoming events** — a carousel of the next events on the community calendar. Click "Show all events" to see the full calendar.

If a section is empty, a prompt will guide you to the relevant directory page.

## Step 1 — Complete your profile

A complete profile helps other members find you and understand your background. Head to **Settings** from the navigation menu, then select **General**.

Things to fill in:

- **First name** and **Last name** — these appear on your public member card
- **Avatar** — upload a profile photo so others can recognise you
- **Bio** — a short description of your yoga journey, practice, or interests
- **Location** — your city and country; this powers the map view in the member directory
- **Social links** — Instagram, YouTube, and website links appear on your public profile

Click **Save Changes** when you are done. Your profile is visible to other members in the directory immediately.

## Step 2 — Find teachers and members

Once your profile is set up, explore the community. Go to **Members** in the main navigation.

The directory shows every GOYA member with their role, location, and designations. You can:

- Filter by role — **Teacher**, **Student**, **School**, or **Wellness Practitioner**
- Filter by country using the country dropdown
- Switch to the **Map** view to browse members geographically
- Click any member card to view their full profile

If you find a teacher you want to learn from, you can send them a **Request Mentorship** from their profile page.

See [Finding Teachers](./finding-teachers.md) for the full guide.

---

## Step 3 — Explore events

Go to **Events** in the main navigation to see the full community calendar.

Events include workshops, teacher trainings, dharma talks, conferences, and more. You can filter by:

- **Date** — pick a specific day using the mini calendar on the left (desktop) or the date picker at the top (mobile)
- **Category** — narrow down by type, such as Workshop or Dharma Talk
- **Event Type** — choose between **GOYA Events** (organised by the GOYA team) and **Member Events** (submitted by teachers and schools)

Click any event to view its full details including time, location, instructor, and price.

See [Events Guide](./events-guide.md) for the full guide.

---

## Step 4 — Browse the Academy

Go to **Academy** in the navigation to access the GOYA course library. Courses are video-based and cover a range of topics: yoga sequences, dharma talks, music playlists, workshops, and research.

Use the filter bar at the top to browse by:

- **Category** — such as Workshop, Yoga Sequence, or Dharma Talk
- **Type** — **GOYA Courses** (curated by GOYA) or **Member Courses** (submitted by teachers)

Click any course card to view its overview page. From there you can enrol and start learning.

See [Academy Guide](./academy-guide.md) for the full guide.

## Step 5 — Get to know your Settings

Your **Settings** page is the central hub for managing your account. Access it from the navigation menu. You will find:

- **General** — edit your profile information, bio, avatar, social links, and appearance preferences
- **Subscriptions** — manage your GOYA membership plan and billing
- **Connections** — view and manage your network of connections
- **Inbox** — check notifications and messages from other members
- **Help** — contact support or ask the AI assistant a question
