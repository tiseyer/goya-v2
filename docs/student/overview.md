# Student Role Overview

Welcome to GOYA — a global community for yoga practitioners, teachers, and schools. As a student member, you are part of a network built to deepen your practice and connect you with qualified teachers around the world.

## What students can do

| Feature | Available to students |
|---|---|
| Complete your profile (avatar, bio, location) | Yes |
| Browse the member directory | Yes |
| View teacher and school profiles | Yes |
| Connect with other members (peer connection) | Yes |
| Request mentorship from a teacher | Yes |
| Browse the event calendar | Yes |
| Register for events | Yes |
| Browse the GOYA Academy course library | Yes |
| Enrol in and complete courses | Yes |
| Track CE credits, karma hours, and practice hours | Yes |
| Submit events for publication | No (teacher role required) |
| Submit courses for publication | No (teacher role required) |
| Log Teaching Hours | No (teacher role required) |

## How the platform is organised

**Dashboard** — Your personalised home page (`/dashboard`). Shows a greeting, a carousel of teachers you are connected with, recently published courses, and upcoming events. Each section links to its full directory page.

**Members** — Browse and connect with the global GOYA community. Find teachers by location, specialty, or designation using the interactive directory and map view.

**Events** — Discover upcoming workshops, teacher trainings, dharma talks, conferences, and more. Events are listed on a calendar with date, category, and type filters.

**Academy** — The GOYA course library. Browse and enrol in video-based courses covering yoga sequences, dharma talks, music playlists, workshops, and research content.

**Settings** — Manage your profile, subscriptions, connections, inbox, and credits all in one place. Access your settings from the navigation menu at any time.

**Credits** — Track your continuing education (CE) credits, karma hours, practice hours, and community contributions at `/credits`.

## Credits and hours

Even as a student, you can log and track your hours on the platform. GOYA recognises several types of contributions:

- **CE Credits** — Continuing education activities
- **Karma Hours** — Service and community contributions
- **Practice Hours** — Your personal yoga practice
- **Community Credits** — Participation in the broader GOYA community

Visit **My Credits & Hours** (`/credits`) to submit and track your progress.

## Upgrading to teacher

If you hold a yoga teaching certification and want to share your work with the GOYA community, you can apply to upgrade your account to the Teacher role. Teachers can submit events, publish courses, and log Teaching Hours.

See [Upgrade to Teacher](./upgrade-to-teacher.md) for details.
