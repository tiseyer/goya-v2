# Events Guide

The GOYA events calendar is where the community shares live learning opportunities — from local workshops to international conferences. This guide explains how to find events, understand the different event types, and register.

## Opening the events page

Click **Events** in the main navigation. The page shows all upcoming published events in chronological order.

---

## GOYA events vs Member events

Every event on GOYA belongs to one of two types:

**GOYA Events** — Organised or curated directly by the GOYA team. These are highlighted with a blue indicator in the **Event Type** filter.

**Member Events** — Submitted by certified teachers and schools in the GOYA community. Member events go through a review before they are published. These appear with an indigo indicator.

You can switch between them using the **Event Type** filter:
- **All Events** — shows both GOYA and Member events (the default)
- **GOYA** — shows only GOYA-organised events
- **Member** — shows only community-submitted events

On desktop, the type filter is in the left sidebar. On mobile, it appears as a segmented control at the top of the page.

## Event categories

Events are organised into the following categories:

| Category | What it covers |
|---|---|
| **Workshop** | Hands-on skill-building sessions |
| **Teacher Training** | Formal training programs and intensives |
| **Dharma Talk** | Philosophy, teachings, and contemplative discussions |
| **Conference** | Multi-session community gatherings |
| **Yoga Sequence** | Sequence-focused practice sessions |
| **Music Playlist** | Music-led or sound-focused events |
| **Research** | Academic and evidence-based presentations |

Each event card and detail page shows a coloured category badge so you can identify the type at a glance.

## Browsing and filtering events

**Filter by date** — On desktop, use the mini calendar in the left sidebar. Days that have events are shown with a small dot beneath the date. Click a highlighted date to filter the list to that day only. Click **Clear date filter** to reset.

On mobile, tap the date button in the filter bar at the top to open the date picker sheet. Tap **Done** when finished.

**Filter by category** — On desktop, click any category in the **Category** section of the left sidebar. On mobile, tap the filter button (the funnel icon) in the filter bar to open the category sheet.

**Filter by type** — Use the **Event Type** filter (desktop sidebar or mobile segmented bar) to switch between All, GOYA, and Member events.

**Combining filters** — All three filters work together. For example, you can show only GOYA Workshops on a specific date. A **Clear filters** button appears in the header row whenever any filter is active.

## Reading an event listing

Each event card in the list shows:

- **Date block** — the day and month on the left-hand side
- **Category badge** and **format badge** (e.g., In-Person, Online)
- **Title** and short description
- **Time** — start and end time
- **Location** — venue or city (for in-person events)
- **Instructor** — the teacher or facilitator running the event
- **Spots remaining** — shown in amber when fewer than 10 spots are left
- **Price** — either a dollar amount or a green **Free** badge

Click anywhere on the card to open the full event detail page.

---

## Viewing an event detail page

The event detail page gives you the complete picture:

- **Hero section** — event title, category, format, and date
- **About This Event** — full description from the organiser
- **Date & Time** — exact start and end times
- **Location** — full address or online link (if provided by the organiser)
- **Instructor** — name of the person leading the event
- **Price and registration** — a booking card on the right side (desktop) or at the bottom (mobile)

A **Back to Events** link at the top of the page takes you back to the calendar without losing your filter settings.

---

## Registering for an event

Registration details and links are provided by each event's organiser. When an event has external registration, you will see a registration button or link on the event detail page — clicking it will take you to the organiser's booking page.

For free events, the event detail page may include direct contact information or instructions from the organiser.

If an event shows **spots remaining**, register early to secure your place. The spots counter updates in real time.

Past events (where the date has already passed) are still accessible in the directory and display a **Past Event** badge, but registration is no longer available.
