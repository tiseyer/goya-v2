# Finding Teachers

The GOYA member directory is where you can discover certified teachers, wellness practitioners, and yoga schools from around the world. This guide explains how to search, filter, and connect.

## Opening the directory

Click **Members** in the main navigation bar. The directory loads with all active GOYA members visible as a scrollable list on the left.

---

## Browsing the list view

Each member card in the list shows:

- **Name** and avatar
- **Location** (city and country)
- **Role badge** — Teacher, Student, School, or Wellness Practitioner
- **Designations** — any certifications or specialties the member has listed
- A short bio excerpt

Click any card to highlight it on the map and open a preview. Click **View Profile** (or click directly to the profile URL) to see the full member page.

---

## Using the map view

The map displays all members who have added a city and country to their profile. It is especially useful when you want to find a teacher near you or in a specific region.

- **Pan and zoom** the map to explore different areas
- **Click a pin** to see that member's card in the list — it will scroll into view and be highlighted
- Clicking the highlighted card opens their profile

The map and list stay in sync — selecting a card on the left moves the map to centre on that member.

---

## Filtering by role, country, and more

Use the sidebar filters (desktop) or the filter panel (mobile) to narrow down the directory.

**Role filter** — Click one of the role chips at the top to show only members with that role:
- **All** — everyone in the directory
- **Teacher** — certified yoga teachers
- **Student** — student practitioners
- **School** — yoga schools and studios
- **Wellness Practitioner** — practitioners outside traditional teacher roles

**Country filter** — Click the **Country** dropdown to select one or more countries. You can type to search for a specific country. Selected countries appear as removable tags below the dropdown.

Additional chip filters (designations, teaching styles, etc.) may appear in the sidebar depending on the member data available. Click any chip to toggle it on or off; click again to remove it.

To reset all filters, remove your selections individually or refresh the page.

---

## Viewing a teacher profile

Click any member card to open their full profile page. A teacher's profile includes:

- **Name**, **role badge**, and **location**
- **About** section — their bio and background
- **Connect** section — links to their website, Instagram, and YouTube (if provided)
- **Connections** — mutual connections you share, if any

Teachers who have filled in their Teaching Profile also display details such as teaching style, lineage, years of experience, and the types of programs they offer.

---

## Connecting with a teacher

On a teacher's profile page, you will see an action button in the right column. The label changes based on your role and theirs:

- **Student visiting a Teacher profile** — the button reads **Request Mentorship**. Clicking it sends a mentorship request to that teacher.
- **Student visiting another Student's profile** — the button reads **Connect**, sending a peer connection request.
- **Student visiting a School profile** — no connection button appears (schools accept faculty applications separately).

After you send a request the button changes to **Mentorship Requested** (or **Request Sent** for peer connections) and becomes inactive until the other member responds.

---

## Understanding connection types

GOYA has two types of connections available to students:

**Peer connection** — A mutual connection between two members of any role. Both members can see each other in their Connections list in Settings.

**Mentorship** — A directed relationship from student to teacher. The teacher receives your request in their **Inbox** and can accept or decline. An accepted mentorship appears on both profiles under Connections.

You can manage all your existing connections from **Settings > Connections**.
