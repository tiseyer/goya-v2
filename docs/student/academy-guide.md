# Academy Guide

The GOYA Academy is the platform's course library — a growing collection of video-based content from the GOYA team and certified community members. This guide explains how to browse, enrol, and track your progress.

## Opening the Academy

Click **Academy** in the main navigation. The page opens with the full course library displayed as a grid of cards.

---

## GOYA courses vs Member courses

Like events, every course in the Academy belongs to one of two types:

**GOYA Courses** — Content produced or curated by the GOYA team. These courses meet GOYA's quality and content standards.

**Member Courses** — Courses submitted by certified teachers and schools. Member submissions go through a review and approval process before they appear in the library.

Use the **Type** filter row beneath the category bar to toggle between **All Courses**, **GOYA Courses**, and **Member Courses**.

---

## Course categories and levels

**Categories** available in the Academy:

| Category | What it covers |
|---|---|
| **Workshop** | Structured skill-building and instructional sessions |
| **Yoga Sequence** | Complete sequences to follow along with |
| **Dharma Talk** | Philosophy, traditions, and contemplative teachings |
| **Music Playlist** | Curated music for practice or teaching |
| **Research** | Academic content, studies, and evidence-based resources |

**Difficulty levels** are shown on each course card:

- **Beginner** — shown in green
- **Intermediate** — shown in amber
- **Advanced** — shown in rose/red
- **All Levels** — suitable for any practitioner

## Browsing and filtering courses

The filter bar sits just below the page hero and stays visible as you scroll.

**Filter by category** — Click any category button in the top row (**All Courses**, **Workshop**, **Yoga Sequence**, **Dharma Talk**, **Music Playlist**, **Research**). The course count in the top right updates to reflect your selection.

**Filter by type** — Use the **Type:** row to switch between **All Courses**, **GOYA Courses**, and **Member Courses**.

**Filter by progress** — If you are logged in, a third row appears: **My Progress**. Use it to quickly find:
- **My Courses** — every course you have enrolled in
- **In Progress** — courses you have started but not yet finished
- **Completed** — courses you have marked as done

All three filter rows work together. Combine them to find, for example, all Beginner Yoga Sequences you have not yet started.

## Reading a course card

Each card in the grid shows:

- **Thumbnail** — a colour gradient with a category icon; completed courses show a green **Completed** badge in the top left
- **Access badge** — **Free** (green) or **Members Only** (dark) in the top right
- **Category badge** — shown at the bottom of the thumbnail
- **Title** and **instructor name**
- **Short description**
- **Duration** and **level** in the card footer
- **Progress bar** — visible on cards for courses you have started or completed

---

## The course overview page

Click any course card to open its detail page. Here you will find:

- Full course description and what the course covers
- Instructor information
- Duration, level, and category
- An enrolment card with the **Start Course** (or **Continue** / **Completed**) button

---

## Enrolling in a course

On the course overview page, click **Start Course** (or the equivalent enrolment button). This records your enrolment and marks the course as **In Progress** in your library.

You will be taken directly to the first lesson of the course. Lessons can include video content, audio recordings, or written text depending on the course.

**Navigating lessons** — A lesson bar appears at the top of each lesson page showing your position (e.g., "Lesson 2 of 5"). Use the **previous** and **next** arrow buttons on the right side of the bar to move between lessons in order.

If you leave before finishing, your progress is saved. Return to the Academy, filter by **In Progress**, and click the course to pick up where you left off.

---

## Tracking your progress

Your progress across all enrolled courses is tracked automatically. You can see it in two places:

**On the Academy page** — Enrolled courses show a progress bar at the bottom of their card:
- A partial bar indicates **In Progress**
- A full bar (100%) confirms **Completed**

**Using the My Progress filters** — Switch to **My Courses**, **In Progress**, or **Completed** in the filter bar to see a focused view of your learning history.

When you finish a course, mark it as complete using the button on the course page. The card badge updates to **Completed** and the course moves to your Completed list.

---

## Free vs Members Only courses

Courses display one of two access levels:

**Free** — Available to all logged-in GOYA members at no cost.

**Members Only** — Requires an active GOYA membership subscription. If you see a Members Only course you want to access, go to **Settings > Subscriptions** to view available membership plans.
