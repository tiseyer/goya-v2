# Upgrading to Teacher

If you hold a yoga teaching certification and want to contribute to the GOYA community as a teacher, you can apply to upgrade your account from Student to Teacher. This page gives you a brief overview of what changes, what is required, and how the process works.

## What changes with the Teacher role

Upgrading to Teacher unlocks a set of features that are not available to students:

**Submit events** — Share your workshops, teacher trainings, dharma talks, and other offerings on the GOYA community calendar. Submitted events go through a review before they are published.

**Submit courses** — Publish video-based courses to the GOYA Academy. Like events, course submissions are reviewed before appearing in the library.

**Log Teaching Hours** — Track the hours you spend actively teaching classes, workshops, private sessions, and intensives. Teaching Hours are a separate credit type from the CE, karma, and practice hours available to all members.

**Teaching Profile** — Your settings page gains a full **Teaching Profile** section where you can document your teaching style, lineage, years of experience, teaching focus areas, and program types. This information appears on your public member profile and makes it easier for students to find you.

The table below summarises the difference:

| Feature | Student | Teacher |
|---|---|---|
| Browse events and courses | Yes | Yes |
| Enrol in courses | Yes | Yes |
| Track CE, karma, and practice credits | Yes | Yes |
| Submit events for publication | No | **Yes** |
| Submit courses for publication | No | **Yes** |
| Log Teaching Hours | No | **Yes** |
| Teaching Profile on member page | No | **Yes** |

## Requirements

To upgrade to the Teacher role you need:

- **A valid yoga teaching certificate** — GOYA requires documentary proof of a recognised certification before granting the Teacher role.
- **An active GOYA account** — You must be signed in to apply.

The specific certificates and training programs recognised by GOYA are listed in the full upgrade guide linked below.

---

## The upgrade process

The upgrade is handled through your account settings. In brief:

1. Go to **Settings** from the navigation menu.
2. Locate the **Upgrade to Teacher** section (visible to Student accounts).
3. Follow the steps to submit your certificate details and any required documentation.
4. The GOYA team reviews your application and will notify you via your **Inbox** when it is approved or if further information is needed.

For step-by-step instructions, screenshots, and answers to common questions, see the dedicated guide:

**[Full Upgrade to Teacher Guide](../teacher/upgrade-guide.md)**

---

## After the upgrade

Once your application is approved your role badge changes to **Teacher** immediately. You will see new sections appear in your settings sidebar:

- **My Events** — create and manage your event submissions
- **My Courses** — create and manage your course submissions
- **Teaching Hours** — log your active teaching time

Your public member profile is also updated automatically to reflect the Teacher role and any Teaching Profile information you add.
