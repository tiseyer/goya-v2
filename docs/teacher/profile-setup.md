## Getting to your settings

Click **Settings** in the main navigation. The settings sidebar opens on the left. Click **General** to reach your profile settings. The sidebar can be collapsed using the arrow icon at the top if you need more space.

---

## Profile Information

The **Profile Information** section is the foundation of your public presence on GOYA.

### Name and tagline

Fill in your **First Name** and **Last Name**. These appear on your member profile and anywhere your content is attributed.

The **Introduction** field (up to 120 characters) is a short tagline shown beneath your name — for example, "Hatha teacher and wellness coach in Berlin." Keep it concise and descriptive.

### Video Introduction

Paste a **YouTube URL** in the **Video Introduction** field to embed a short welcome video on your profile. This is optional but helps community members get to know you.

### Bio

The **Bio** field (up to 500 characters) gives you space to describe your background, approach, and what you offer the community. Write in the first person and keep it welcoming.

### Location

Enter your **Location** in "City, Country" format. This is visible on your public profile.

### Social links

Connect your online presence using the **Website**, **Instagram**, and **YouTube** fields. Enter your full website URL, your Instagram handle (with or without the @ symbol), and your YouTube channel name.

Click **Save Changes** when you are done with this section.

## Teaching Profile

If your role is **Teacher**, you will see a **Teaching Profile** section below the main profile form. This section does not appear for students.

Fill in the following fields to help other members understand your background:

- **Years Teaching** — select a range from the dropdown (e.g. "5-10 years")
- **Teaching Format** — choose In-Person, Online, or Hybrid
- **Influences** — select what has shaped your practice
- **Teaching Styles** — choose the styles you teach (e.g. Hatha Yoga, Yin Yoga, Vinyasa Yoga)
- **Teaching Focus** — select your primary areas of focus (e.g. Breath Work, Flexibility and Mobility)
- **Lineage** — select up to 3 lineages that inform your teaching

Changes save along with the main profile when you click **Save Changes**.

School owners see a **School Profile** section instead, with fields for programs offered, year established, and course delivery format.

---

## Account Information

The **Account Information** section shows read-only details about your membership:

- **Member Number (MRN)** — your unique GOYA member reference number
- **Member Since** — the month and year you joined
- **Role** — your current role (e.g. Teacher, Wellness Practitioner)

You can update your **Email Address** here. Click **Update** after changing it — a verification email will be sent to your new address before the change takes effect.

---

## Appearance

The **Appearance** section lets you choose your preferred color theme for the GOYA interface. Select the theme you prefer and it will apply immediately.

---

## What other members see

Your public member profile displays:

- Your name and Introduction tagline
- Your avatar (initials shown until avatar upload is available)
- Your Bio
- Your Location
- Your Teaching Profile details (styles, focus, lineage, years teaching)
- Links to your Website, Instagram, and YouTube

Your email address, Member Number, and account settings are private and never shown to other members.
