# Help & AI Support

The Help page (Settings > Help) provides instant answers via Mattea, the AI support assistant. You can ask questions, browse previous conversations, and start new chats at any time.

---

## Asking a Question

Type your question into the input field and press Enter or the send button. Mattea replies immediately by streaming a response.

Once a conversation has started, a follow-up input appears below the chat so you can continue the thread.

---

## Conversation History

If you have had previous help conversations, a **Previous Conversations** dropdown appears below the chat area. It shows your last 20 help-page conversations, most recent first, with a short preview of what you asked and how long ago it was.

Click any conversation to load the full message thread.

Loaded conversations are **read-only** — you cannot send new messages in a historical view. A note below the input area indicates you are viewing a previous conversation.

---

## Starting a New Chat

Click the refresh icon in the chat header to start a fresh conversation. Your previous conversations are not deleted — they continue to appear in the dropdown so you can refer back to them at any time.

---

## Escalation

If Mattea cannot resolve your issue, it will offer to connect you with a human support team member. A support ticket is created automatically and you will hear back via the platform.
