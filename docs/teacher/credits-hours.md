## What are GOYA credits?

The GOYA credits program lets you track your professional development, personal practice, community contributions, and active teaching time — all in one place. Credits provide a record of your commitment to continuous growth and are visible on your dashboard at **/credits**.

---

## Credit types

GOYA tracks five types of credits and hours:

| Type | Who can earn it | What it tracks |
|---|---|---|
| **CE Credits** | All members | Continuing Education — courses, trainings, workshops, seminars |
| **Karma Hours** | All members | Community service, volunteer teaching, doing good |
| **Practice Hours** | All members | Personal yoga practice sessions |
| **Teaching Hours** | Teachers only | Hours spent actively teaching classes, workshops, or private sessions |
| **Community Engagement Credits** | All members | Earned automatically through platform activity (posts, comments, event attendance, course completions, likes received) |

Community Engagement Credits are awarded automatically — you do not need to submit them. All other types require a manual submission, except when an activity is tracked directly through the platform.

---

## Your credits dashboard

Go to **/credits** to see your full credits picture:

- A **status card** for each credit type showing your current total and whether you are on track
- An **overall status banner** summarizing your standing
- A **Submit New Credits** panel for logging new activities
- A **Credits History** table showing all past submissions

If your account has a **Teaching Hours** requirement, a progress bar shows how close you are to meeting it within the required time window.

Teachers also have a dedicated **My Teaching Hours** page at **/teaching-hours** with a focused view and a quick log form.

## Submitting credits manually

Use the **Submit New Credits** panel on the credits page (**/credits**) to log a new activity. The form walks you through five steps:

1. **Intro** — a summary of submission rules. Each submission should represent one single session, course, or event. Click **Next** to continue.
2. **Choose Category** — select the credit type that matches your activity: CE Credits, Karma Hours, Practice Hours, or (for teachers) Teaching Hours.
3. **How many hours?** — enter the number of hours using the plus and minus buttons, or type directly into the field. Minimum 0.5, increments of 0.5, maximum 250 per submission.
4. **When did this take place?** — pick the date from the inline calendar. Activities must be within the last two years.
5. **Describe the Activity** — write a description of the activity so the GOYA team can verify it. Include the instructor name, location, and any relevant context. Up to 3,000 characters.

Click **Submit Activity** on the final step to send your submission for review.

---

## Logging Teaching Hours

Teachers have two ways to log Teaching Hours:

1. On the main credits page (**/credits**), open the **Submit New Credits** panel and choose **Teaching Hours** as the category.
2. Go directly to **/teaching-hours** and use the **Log Teaching Hours** panel, which skips the category selection step.

The teaching hours form is the same five-step flow, with the category pre-selected as Teaching Hours.

---

## The approval process

All manual submissions go through a review by the GOYA team. After you submit:

- Your submission appears in your **Credits History** with a **Pending Review** status.
- Submissions are typically reviewed within **5 business days**.
- Once approved, the status changes to **Approved** and the hours are added to your running totals.
- If a submission is rejected, the status changes to **Rejected**. The rejection reason is visible in your history.

Your dashboard totals only reflect approved credits.

---

## Expiration rules

Credits are valid for **one year from the date of the activity** — not the date you submitted them.

Credits approaching or past their expiration date are shown with reduced opacity in your Credits History table and flagged on your dashboard. Expired credits no longer count toward your active totals or any requirements.

---

## Viewing your history

The **Credits History** table at the bottom of your dashboard (**/credits**) shows all your submissions in reverse chronological order. Each row shows:

- **Date** — the date the activity took place
- **Type** — CE Credits, Karma Hours, Practice Hours, Teaching Hours, or Community Credits
- **Amount** — number of hours or credits
- **Description** — your activity description
- **Status** — Approved, Pending Review, or Rejected
- **Expires** — expiration date (if applicable)

The table pages 25 entries at a time. Use **Previous** and **Next** to navigate.

On the teaching hours page (**/teaching-hours**), the history table is filtered to show only Teaching Hours entries.
