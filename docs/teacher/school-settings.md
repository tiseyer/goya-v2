# School Settings

Once your school has completed onboarding, you can manage all of its details from the School Settings area.

## Accessing School Settings

After your school is registered, a **School Settings** link appears in the header dropdown menu (between Settings and Register School). Click it to open the settings area for your school.

Direct URL: `/schools/[your-school-slug]/settings`

## Sidebar Navigation

The settings area has a collapsible left sidebar with eight sections:

| Section | What You Can Do |
|---------|----------------|
| **General** | Update name, URL slug, short bio, full bio, and established year |
| **Online Presence** | Set website, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, and video intro links |
| **Teaching Info** | Update practice styles, programs offered, delivery format, lineage, and languages |
| **Location** | Change your school's address using Google Places autocomplete |
| **Faculty** | Manage faculty members and their positions |
| **Designations** | View your active designations |
| **Documents** | View and re-upload verification documents per designation |
| **Subscription** | View your school subscription and billing information |

Click the arrow button at the top of the sidebar to collapse it to icon-only mode.

## Pending Review Banner

If your school's status is **pending review**, a yellow banner appears at the top of the settings area:

> "Your school is currently under review. This can take up to 1 week."

This happens when your school was first submitted for admin approval, or when you change the school's **name** or **URL slug** — both trigger a re-review.

## Name and Slug Changes

Changing your school's name or slug in General settings will automatically set your school's status back to **pending review**. Admin staff will review the change before it appears on your public school profile.

Other changes (bio, online presence, teaching info, location, faculty) do not trigger a re-review.

## Inviting Faculty by Email

From the **Faculty** tab, you can invite a non-GOYA-member to join your school as faculty. Enter their email address and position title, then click **Invite**. They will receive an invitation email containing a registration link.

When they create their GOYA account using that link, they are automatically added as an active faculty member of your school — no further action is needed from you.

Invited members appear in your faculty list with a **Pending** badge until they register.
