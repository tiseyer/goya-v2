# Register Your School on GOYA

If you own or operate a yoga school, you can list it on GOYA to manage designations, connect with students, and grow your school's presence on the platform.

## Where to Find the Registration Link

As a teacher without a registered school, you will see a **Register Your School** prompt in three places:

- **Dashboard** — right sidebar widget titled "Own a Yoga School?"
- **Settings > Subscriptions** — callout below your membership card
- **Add-Ons & Upgrades** — banner above the product grid

Each prompt includes a **Register Your School** button that takes you to `/schools/create`.

## Who Sees These Prompts

- **Teachers without a school** — all three prompts are visible
- **Teachers who already own a school** — prompts are hidden
- **Students and wellness practitioners** — prompts are not shown

## Registration Wizard

The registration flow is a 3-step guided wizard:

### Step 1: School Name

Enter your school name (minimum 3 characters). A URL slug is generated automatically — for example, "Sunrise Yoga Academy" becomes `goya.org/schools/sunrise-yoga-academy`. You can edit the slug directly. A green checkmark confirms the URL is available; a red X means it is already taken.

### Step 2: Choose Your Designations

Select one or more GOYA school designations your school will offer:

| Designation | Full Name |
|-------------|-----------|
| GOYA-CYS200 | GOYA Certified Yoga School 200 |
| GOYA-CYS300 | GOYA Certified Yoga School 300 |
| GOYA-CYS500 | GOYA Certified Yoga School 500 |
| GOYA-CCYS | GOYA Certified Children's Yoga School |
| GOYA-CPYS | GOYA Certified Prenatal Yoga School |
| GOYA-CYYS | GOYA Certified Yin Yoga School |
| GOYA-CRYS | GOYA Certified Restorative Yoga School |
| GOYA-CMS | GOYA Certified Meditation School |

**Pricing per designation:**
- EUR 40.00/year (annual subscription, renewable)
- EUR 99.00 one-time signup fee

A running total at the bottom updates as you select. You can add more designations later — you don't need to select everything now.

### Step 3: Payment

Click **Continue to Payment** to proceed to Stripe Checkout. Your school details and selections are saved as a draft in your browser, so you can safely navigate back if needed.

After successful payment, you will be redirected to your school's onboarding flow to complete your profile.

## Your progress is saved

If you close your browser mid-registration or Stripe returns you to the previous page, your school name, slug, and selected designations are restored automatically from your browser's local storage.
