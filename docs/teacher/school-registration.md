# Register Your School on GOYA

If you own or operate a yoga school, you can list it on GOYA to manage designations, connect with students, and grow your school's presence on the platform.

## Where to Find the Registration Link

As a teacher without a registered school, you will see a **Register Your School** prompt in three places:

- **Dashboard** — right sidebar widget titled "Own a Yoga School?"
- **Settings > Subscriptions** — callout below your membership card
- **Add-Ons & Upgrades** — banner above the product grid

Each prompt includes a **Register Your School** button that takes you to `/schools/create`.

## Who Sees These Prompts

- **Teachers without a school** — all three prompts are visible
- **Teachers who already own a school** — prompts are hidden
- **Students and wellness practitioners** — prompts are not shown

## Getting Started

Click **Register Your School** from any of the three entry points to begin the school registration flow.
