## Who this guide is for

This guide applies to anyone on GOYA with a **Teacher**, **Wellness Practitioner**, or **School Owner** role. All three roles share the same elevated feature set — the teacher-specific settings sections and submission workflows are available to all of them.

If you are currently a student and want to become a teacher on GOYA, see [Upgrade to Teacher](./upgrade-guide.md).

---

## What teachers can do

Teachers have all the same capabilities as students, plus additional tools for sharing their work with the GOYA community.

| Feature | Student | Teacher |
|---|---|---|
| Browse the event calendar | Yes | Yes |
| Browse the academy | Yes | Yes |
| Submit credits and hours | Yes | Yes |
| Submit events for publication | No | **Yes** |
| Submit courses for publication | No | **Yes** |
| Log Teaching Hours | No | **Yes** |
| Display Teaching Profile on member page | No | **Yes** |

---

## Features exclusive to teachers

### My Events

Submit workshops, teacher trainings, dharma talks, and other events to the GOYA community calendar. Events go through a review process before they are published. Go to **Settings > My Events** to get started.

### My Courses

Submit video-based courses to the GOYA Academy — including yoga sequences, dharma talks, music playlists, and research content. Courses follow the same review process as events. Go to **Settings > My Courses** to get started.

### Teaching Hours

Log the hours you spend actively teaching classes, workshops, and private sessions. Teaching Hours are tracked separately from your other credit types and count toward any active teaching requirements on your account. Go to **/teaching-hours** to log hours.

### Teaching Profile

Your settings page includes a **Teaching Profile** section where you can document your teaching style, lineage, years of experience, and teaching format. This information appears on your public member profile and helps students and other practitioners find you.

## Settings navigation

Your settings sidebar includes the following sections. Teacher-only items are marked below:

- **General** — profile information, account details, appearance
- **Subscriptions** — billing and membership management
- **Connections** — manage your network
- **Inbox** — notifications and messages
- **My Events** — (teachers only) submit and manage events
- **My Courses** — (teachers only) submit and manage courses
- **Help** — contact support or use the AI assistant
