## Who can upgrade

Any GOYA member with a **Student** or **Wellness Practitioner** role can apply to upgrade to **GOYA Certified Teacher**. The upgrade requires valid teaching credentials and a yearly membership fee.

---

## What changes after approval

Once your application is approved your account role changes to **Teacher**. You will gain access to:

- **My Events** — submit workshops, trainings, and other events to the community calendar
- **My Courses** — submit courses to the GOYA Academy
- **Teaching Hours** — log and track your active teaching time
- **Teaching Profile** — display your teaching styles, lineage, and experience on your member profile
- Exclusive teacher resources and materials
- Your credentials displayed on your public GOYA profile
- Connection to the broader GOYA teacher community

## The upgrade process

Go to **/upgrade** to begin. The process has three steps.

---

### Step 1 — Review the benefits and pricing

The first screen shows what you gain with a teacher membership and the annual fee:

**Teacher Membership — $39.00 / year**

Read through the listed benefits. When you are ready, click **Start Upgrade** to move to the next step.

---

### Step 2 — Upload your certificates

Upload between 1 and 3 certificate files that verify your teaching qualifications. Accepted file types are **PDF, JPG, PNG, and WEBP**, with a maximum size of **4 MB per file**.

To add a file:
1. Click **Add Certificate**.
2. Select your file from your device. The file uploads immediately and appears in the list.
3. Repeat for additional certificates (up to 3 total).

To remove a file that you added, click **Remove** next to the file name.

Once at least one certificate is uploaded, the **Continue to Payment** button becomes active. Click it to proceed.

Click **Back** at any time to return to the previous step without losing what you have already uploaded.

---

### Step 3 — Authorize payment

The final step confirms the payment details:

**Teacher Membership — $39.00 / year**

Note: **Your payment is not charged at this point.** The charge only occurs after the GOYA team has verified your credentials.

The screen also confirms how many certificate files are ready to send.

Click **Proceed to Checkout** to be redirected to the secure Stripe payment page. Complete payment on the Stripe page to finalize your submission.

---

## After you submit

After completing checkout, you land on a confirmation page with the message:

> "Your upgrade request has been submitted! Our team will verify your credentials within 48 hours."

What happens next:

1. The GOYA team reviews your certificates within **48 hours**.
2. If approved, your role changes to **Teacher**, your teacher features become available, and your annual membership is charged.
3. You will receive a notification once the review is complete.

If there is an issue with your credentials, the team will reach out to let you know.

---

## Common questions

**Can I edit my certificates after submitting?**
No. Once you complete checkout, the submission is final. If you need to provide different certificates, contact GOYA support.

**What if my application is not approved?**
The team will contact you. Your payment is not charged if your credentials are not verified.

**Can I cancel my teacher membership?**
Yes. Go to **Settings > General** and use the **End Subscription** option in the Danger Zone. Your teacher access continues until the end of the current billing period.
